Frequent Questions

These are some of the most frequently asked questions.

What is the participation amount?

Minimum activation, $130,000. Every project will depend on the dimension that the associate wants to give to the construction; as well as the use permitted by the acquired land (single-family house, duplex or multifamily).

How do you participate in the business?

- With the acquisition of the land (cash payment, not financing).
- With the contribution of the capital necessary to start construction.
- Linking with AMERIDEVE through a joint venture and a listing agreement.
- Establishing a company (LLC) in the US, which will own the project to be developed.

Where does the economic resource go?

To a bank account in the name of the company (LLC), opened by the client. In this account will be the resources that will be used throughout the construction, at the disposal of the associated builder.

Who carries out the project ans processes permits?

AMERIDEVE has architects and a team with experience in both topics, so AMERIDEVE is in charge of processing the permit and developing the project.

How does the member obtains financing?

As part of the management, AMERIDEVE presents the project to the financier for an evaluation, and determination of the percentage and other terms for the financing that corresponds to the total value of the project.

At what moment does the suppor ends?

AMERIDEVE is a real estate development concept that articulates all the factors present in the business model.
Our support is throughout the entire process.
